
# Lecture 1 Wave 

#### Wave
$$
y(x,t)=Acos(kx-wt+\theta)
$$
#### Amplitude and Intensity
$$
I_{average}=\frac{1}{T}\int^{T}_{0}|A|^{2}|cos(kx-wt+\theta)|^2dt=\frac{|A|^2}{2}
$$
$$
I=\frac{A^2}{2}
$$
